About Us

Founded in 1995, Gulu Institution of Agriculture is dedicated to providing comprehensive agricultural education, blending theoretical knowledge with practical experience. Our mission is to shape skilled professionals ready to drive innovation, sustainability, and productivity in agriculture.

Programs Offered

National Diploma in Animal Production and Management

  • Duration: 2 Years, Full-time
  • Subjects: Livestock Management, Animal Health, Breeding, Nutrition
  • Entry: O-Level Certificate

National Diploma in Crop Production and Management

  • Duration: 2 Years, Full-time
  • Subjects: Crop Science, Soil Management, Pest Control, Irrigation
  • Entry: O-Level Certificate

National Certificate in Agriculture Production and Management

  • Duration: 1 Year, Full-time
  • Subjects: Crop Production, Animal Production, Farm Management
  • Entry: O-Level Certificate

Short Courses

  • Poultry Management (1 month)
  • Greenhouse Farming (2 months)
  • Beekeeping (1 month)
  • Organic Farming (6 weeks)
