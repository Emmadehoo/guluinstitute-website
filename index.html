About Our Institution

Established in 2012, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive agricultural education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills.

Excellence in Agricultural Education

Gulu Institution of Agriculture is dedicated to providing comprehensive agricultural education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills. Our institution has been at the forefront of agricultural training in Uganda for over 13 years.

We pride ourselves on our experienced faculty, modern facilities, and extensive farmland that serves as a living laboratory for our students. Our graduates are well-equipped to tackle the challenges of modern agriculture and contribute to food security and sustainable development.
